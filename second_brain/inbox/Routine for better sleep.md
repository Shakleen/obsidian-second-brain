> [!Error] Caution
If you sleep less than the recommended **7 hours**, you risk cutting REM sleep stage essential for brain development and maintenance. ([[Optimize Sleep Quality for Busy Schedules#C. The Challenge of Sleep Compression and Architecture|Source]])

> [!Success] Do
> 1. Execise
>    - Hard / moderate: Early Morning
>    - Light: Closer to bed time.
> 2. Warm shower close to bed time
> 3. Cold shower after waking up
> 4. Dim lights **1 hour** prior to sleeping
> 5. Sleep in increments of 1.5 hours 
>    - Ideally 7.5 hours (*5 X 1.5 hours*)
>    - Minimum, 6 hours (*4 X 1.5 hours*)

> [!Error] Don't
> 1. Caffeine within 6 hours wihtin bed time
> 2. Heavy meals 3 hours within 3 hours of bed time.
> 3. Heavy workout or stressful work 2 hours before bed time.
> 4. Bright lights or screens 1 hours before bed time
> 5. Sleeping less than 6 hours
