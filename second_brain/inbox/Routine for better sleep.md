> [!Info]- Before Sleeping
> > [!Success] Do
> > 1. 1.5 hours before: Warm shower
> > 2. 1 hour before: Dim warm lights
> > 3. 10 minutes before: Mindfulness exercise.
>
> > [!Error] Don't
> > 1. 6 hours before: No caffeine.
> > 2. 3 hours before: No heavy meals
> > 3. 2 hours before: No stressful work or exercise
> > 4. 1 hour before: No screens

> [!Info]- After Waking
> > [!Success] Do
> > 1. Immediately: Raise heart rate by doing some exercise.
> > 2. Within 10 minutes: Cold shower
> > 3. Within 30 minutes: Bright light (Ideally sun light)
> 
> > [!Error] Don't
> > 1. Warm shower
> > 2. Warm or dim lights

> [!Info] Schedule
> Consistent sleeping schedule
