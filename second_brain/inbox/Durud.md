Said during the last [[Tashahud]] of [[2025-10-28_Salat|Salah]] after [[Attahyyatu]] and before [[Closing Supplication]].
## Verses
1. Allaahumma salli ‘ala Muhammad wa ‘ala Aali Muhammad
   اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ، وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
   > O Allah! Send Your Mercy on Muhammad and on the family of Muhammad,
2. kama salayta ‘ala Ibrahim wa ‘ala aali Ibraaheem
   مَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ
   > as You sent Your Mercy on Abraham and on the family of Abraham, 
3. innaka hameedun majeed
   إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ
   > for You are the Most Praise-worthy, the Most Glorious.
4. Allahumma baarik ‘ala Muhammad wa ‘ala Aali Muhammad
   اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ، وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
   > O Allah! Send Your Blessings on Muhammad and the family of Muhammad,
5. kama baarakta ‘ala Ibrahim wa ála aali Ibraaheem
   كَمَا بَارَكْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ، وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ
   > as You sent your Blessings on Abraham and on the family of Abraham,
6. innaka hameedun majeed.
   إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ
   > for You are the Most Praise-worthy, the Most Glorious.'

## Quranic Verses
> “Allah sends His Salât (Graces, Honours, Blessings, Mercy) on the Prophet and also His angels (ask Allah to bless and forgive him). O you who believe! Send your Salât on (ask Allah to bless him), and (you should) greet (salute) him with the Islamic way of greeting (salutation i.e. As¬Salâmu 'Alaikum)” \[al-Ahzaab 33:56\].
