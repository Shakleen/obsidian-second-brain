## [[Amal]] of [[Omrah]]
What are the 4 [[Amal|Amals]] of [[Omrah]]?
?
1. [[Ehram]]
2. [[Tawaf-e-Omrah]]
3. [[Sayi]]
4. [[Halk]] or [[Kasar]]

How many [[Amal|Amals]] in [[Omrah]] are essential or obligatory?
?
Four

What are the 2 [[Fards of Omrah]]?
?
1. [[Ehram]]
2. [[Tawaf-e-Omrah]]

How many [[Fards of Omrah]]?
?
Two

To perform [[Ehram]] in [[Omrah]] is a ==[[Fard]]==^[Fard or Wajib] [[Amal]].

To perform [[Tawaf-e-Omrah]] in [[Omrah]] is a ==[[Fard]]==^[Fard or Wajib] [[Amal]].

What are the 2 [[Wajibs of Omrah]]?
?
1. [[Sayi]]
2. [[Halk]] or [[Kasar]]

How many [[Wajibs of Omrah]]?
?
Two

To perform [[Sayi]] in [[Omrah]] is a ==[[Wajib]]==^[Fard or Wajib] [[Amal]].

To perform [[Halk]] in [[Omrah]] is a ==[[Wajib]]==^[Fard or Wajib] [[Amal]].

To perform [[Kasar]] in [[Omrah]] is a ==[[Wajib]]==^[Fard or Wajib] [[Amal]].
