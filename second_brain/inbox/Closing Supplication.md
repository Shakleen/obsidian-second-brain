Said in [[Tashahud]] in the final [[Rakat]] after [[Durud]] and before [[Taslim]] to end the [[2025-10-28_Salat|Salah]]. This isn't a single supplication but a list of many. Find more in [[@aminIntroductionSalatPerformed2010]] book.
## Supplication #1
This supplication was taught to Hazrat Abu Bakar (RA) by the Prophet (SA).
1. Allahhumma Inni Zalamtu Nafsi julman Kaseeran,
   اَللّٰھُمَّ أِنِّیْ ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِیْ ظُلْمًا کَثِیْرًا
   > O Allah I have done great wrong to myself.
2. Wa la Yaghfiruz-Zunooba Illa Anta
    وَّلَا یَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوْبَ اِلَّا أَنْتَ 
    > and none, but You only, can forgive my sins.
3. Faghfirlee Maghfiratan-min ‘Indika warhamni
   فَاغْفِرْلِیْ مَغْفِرَةً مِّنْ عِنْدِكَ وَارْحَمْنِیْ 
   > Forgive me, excuse me of Your's
4. Innakaa Antal ghafoorur Ra’heem
   أِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْغَفُوْرُ الرَّحِیْمَ
   > and be kind on me, varily you are the most kind and most merciful.
