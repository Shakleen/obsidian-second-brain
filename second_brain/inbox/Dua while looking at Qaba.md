1. Raise your hands to your shoulders <span style="color:red;">(not up to ear height)</span>
2. Say the following 3 times out loud:
   > Alhamdulillahi allahu akbar, la ilaha illallahu
   > Meaning: All praise only for Allah. He is almighty. There is no god except for Him.
