# Salah [[Sunnat-e-Muakkadah|Sunnahs]]
1. [[Adhan]]
2. Raise hands during [[Takbir e Tahrima]]
3. Have hands face the Qibla during [[Takbir e Tahrima]]
4. Imam says [[Takbir]] out loud
5. Placing right hand over left
6. Recite [[Sana]]
7. Saying [[2025-11-03_Auzubillah|Auzubillah]]
8. Saying [[Bismillah]]
9. Saying Amin
10. Saying 8, 9, and 10 silently
11. [[Takbir]] when going to and standing up from Ruku
12. [[Ruku Tasbih]]
13. [[Shijda Tasbih]]
14. Placing hand on knee during [[Ruku]]
15. When going to [[Shijda]] first knees, then hands, then forehead, and finally nose should touch the ground
16. Males should sit on their left leg and keep right leg up right during [[Tashahud]]. Females should keep both legs to their left side
17. Keep arms on your thighs during [[Tashahud]]
18. During [[Shahada]] point with your index
19. Reciting [[Durud]]
20. [[Munajat]]
21. [[Salam]] begins from right
22. [[Sura Fatiha]] for last 2 [[Rakat|Rakats]] of [[Fard]] [[Salah|salats]]
