2 [[Rakat]] of [[2025-10-28_Salat|Salah]] is performed at the end of [[Tawaf-e-Omrah]]. Doing so is [[Wajib]].

> [!Info] Where
> It's [[Mustahab]] to do this prayer behind [[Makam-e-Ibrahim]].

## Process
1. Start with this dua
  > Wattakhiju mim makami ibrahim musolla
2. Follow the rules of regular Salah. Except for [[Qirat]], after [[Sura Fatiha]] it's good to say the following:
	1. [[Sura Kafirun]] in first [[Rakat]] 
	2. [[Sura Ikhlas]] in second [[Rakat]]
