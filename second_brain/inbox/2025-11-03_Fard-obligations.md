# Fard obligations

## Level: Obligatory
**Meaning**: **Absolute obligations** mandated by Allah in the Qur'an or established through undeniable Mutawatir Hadith.

## Consequence of Omission

### Religious acts
* Intentional: [[Major Sin]].
* Unintentional: Requires [[Qada]] be performed as soon as remembered.
  
### In a process 
Requires the entire process to be repeated again. Otherwise, the process isn't valid.

## Example:

### Religious acts
- 5 daily prayers ([[2025-10-30_Fajr-prayer|Fajr]], [[2025-10-30_Duhr-prayer|Duhr]], [[2025-10-30_Asr-prayer|Asr]], [[2025-10-30_Magrib-prayer|Magrib]], [[2025-10-30_Isha-prayer|Isha]])
- [[Fasting]] during Ramadan
- Giving [[Zakat]]
- etc.
