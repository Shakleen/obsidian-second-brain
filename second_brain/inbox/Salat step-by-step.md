There is no mention of how to perform [[2025-10-28_Salat|Salat]] in the Qur'an. Zibrael (A) appeared before the Prophet (SA) and showed him practically how to perform salat and when to.
## Steps
The following steps should be performed in order and maintain [[Tadeel Arkan]] (doing so is [[Salah Wajibs|Wajib]]).
1. [[Eqama]] for [[Fard]] salah
2. [[Intention of Salat]]
3. [[Takbir e Tahrima]]
	1. Start and hold [[Qiyam]] position
	2. [[Opening Supplication]]
	3. Only for the first [[Rakat]], recite [[2025-11-03_Auzubillah|Auzubillah]]
	4. [[Qirat]]
4. [[Takbir]]
	1. Exit [[Qiyam]] to go to [[Ruku]] position
	2. Recite [[Ruku Tasbih]] minimum 3 times. (You can say more but the count must be odd)
5. [[Ruku Takbir]]
	1. Exit [[Ruku]] to go to [[Qawma]] position.
	2. Respond with [[Ruku supplication]]
6. [[Takbir]]
	1. Exit [[Qawma]] to go to [[Shijda]]. 
	2. Recite [[Shijda Tasbih]] minimum 3 times (You say more but the count must be odd).
7. [[Takbir]] 
	1. Exit [[Shijda]] to go to [[Jalsa]] position
	2. Optional: Say [[Jalsa supplication]]
8. [[Takbir]] 
	1. Exit [[Jalsa]] to go into [[Shijda]] again.
	2. Recite [[Shijda Tasbih]] minimum 3 times (You say more but the count must be odd).
9. [[Takbir]]: If you're in
	1. **(First [[Rakat]])** 
	   Exit [[Shijda]] and go back to [[Qiyam]] position
	2. **(Second [[Rakat]] but not final)** 
		1. Remain seated in [[Tashahud]] position. 
		2. Say [[Attahyyatu]]. 
		3. [[Takbir]] Exit [[Tashahud]] to go to [[QIyam]] position.
	3. **(Final [[Rakat]])** 
		1. Remain seated in [[Tashahud]] position. 
		2. Recite [[Attahyyatu]]
		3. Recite [[Durud]]
		4. Recite [[Closing Supplication]]
		5. End prayer with [[Prayer Salam|Salam]]
		6. Optional: [[Munajat]]
