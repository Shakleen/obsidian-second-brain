1. Take off shoes before entering the mosque.
	* If you're going to take the shoes with you, make sure they are clean.
	* First take off your left shoe, then right.
2. Say [[Bismillah]] before entering.
3.  Recite [[Durud]] and [[Salam]].
4.  Perform [[Niyat]] for [[Nafl]] [[Itekaf]]
