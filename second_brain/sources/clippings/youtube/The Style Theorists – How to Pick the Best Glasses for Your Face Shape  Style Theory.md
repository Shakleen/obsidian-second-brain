## Summary
1. There are mainly 7 different face shapes. [Timestamp](https://youtu.be/w5Fd8c0EUAA?t=119)
   ![[Pasted image 20251110093046.png]]
2. There are 3 measurements you need to take:
	1. Forehead to chin length
	2. Forehead width: Edge of hairline to edge of hairline
	3. Width of jawline: Back of jawbone to center of your chin. Then multiply by 2
3. Rounded frames soften your edges
4. Square or geometric accentuates your edges.
