># Salah Steps

## Fards

1. Takbir e Tahrima
2. Qiyam
3. Qirat
4. Ruku
5. Shijda
6. Shahada

## Wajibs

1. Recite Sura Fatiha
2. Recite one other Sura (at least 3 ayaats)
3. Taâ€™dil Arkan
4. Qirat should be done in the first 2 rakats of Fard prayers
5. Maintain order of Qirat, Ruku, and Shijda
6. First shahada
7. Tashahud
8. Reciting loud or quietly when required
9. Salam to end prayer
10. Reciting Dua Qunood for Bitr salah

## Sunnats

1. Adhan
2. Raise hands during Takbir e tahrima
3. Have hands face the Qibla during Takbir e Tahrima
4. Imam says Takbir out loud
5. Placing right hand over left
6. Place hands right below belly button for males, and on chest for females
7. Recite Sana
8. Saying Auzubillah
9. Saying Bismillah
10. Saying Amin
11. Saying 8, 9, and 10 silently
12. Takbir when going to and standing up from Ruku
13. Tasbih for Ruku and Shijdah
14. Placing hand on knee during ruku
15. When going to Shijda first knees, then hands, then forehead, and finally nose should touch the ground
16. Males should sit on their left leg and keep right leg up right during Tashahud. Females should keep both legs to their left side
17. Keep arms on your thighs during Tashahud
18. During Shahada point with your index
19. Saying Durud
20. Munajat
21. Salam begins from right
22. Sura Fatiha for last 2 rakats of Fard salats.
