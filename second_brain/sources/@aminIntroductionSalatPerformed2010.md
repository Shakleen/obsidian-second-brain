# Notes
>
># Adhan

Abdullah Ibn Omar said that the Prophet (SA) said, “When you hear the Muazzen, say what he says, then ask a blessing for me. Whoever asks a blessing for me once, Allah will bless him ten times. Then ask Allah for his favour to me. He who asks Allah’s favour for me, for him will I intercede.” (Muslim)

# Kinds of Salats

## Fards or Obligatory salats

1. Fajr: 2 rakats
2. Duhr: 4 rakats
3. Asr: 4 rakats
4. Magrib: 3 rakats
5. Isha: 4 rakats

## Wazib salats

1. Bitr: 3 rakats
2. Funeral prayer
3. Eid prayer

## Sunnat

### Sunnat-e-Muakkada

1. Fajr: 2 rakats before Fard
2. Duhr:
    
    1. 4 rakats before Fard
    2. 2 rakats after Fard
3. Magrib: 2 rakats after Fard
4. Isha: 2 rakats after Fard

### Sunnat-e-Gair Muakkada

1. Asr: 4 rakats before Fard
2. Isha: 4 rakats before Fard
3. Bitr: 2 rakats before Bitr

## Nafl

1. Duhr: 2 rakats
2. Magrib: 2 rakats
3. Isha: 2 rakats
4. Bitr: 2 rakats
5. [[Tahajjud prayer]]
6. [[Tahiatul Wudu]]
7. [[Daklul masjid]]
8. [[Salatul Ishraq]]

# Virtues of Wudu

1. Osman (RA) reported, the Prophet (SA) said, “If anybody makes Wudu properly and performs two rakats of salat with complete devotion to Allah, all his previous sins (except kabira sins) are forgiven” - Bukhari
2. Hazrat Anas (RA) reported that Prophet (SA) used to make fresh wudu before performing every salat. - Bukhari.
