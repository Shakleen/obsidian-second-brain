### Imported: 2025-11-05 8:33 am



<mark style="background-color: #ff6666">Quote</mark>
> Ta'dil al-Arkan means to perform the principles (rukns) properly. Ta'dil al-Arkan in prayers means to perform each rukn (principle) of the prayer like qiyam, ruku', sajdah, etc calmly, solemnly and properly, and not to hurry.

<mark style="background-color: #ff6666">Quote</mark>
> It is contrary to ta'dil al-arkan to prostrate immediately after ruku without straightening up properly and to prostrate after the first sajdah without sitting still.

<mark style="background-color: #ff6666">Quote</mark>
> If it is not fulfilled, the prayer is invalidated and it is necessary to perform it again.

<mark style="background-color: #ffd400">Quote</mark>
> There are some scholars who hold the view that it is wajib to perform a prayer again if a person commits more than one makruh deed in a rukn of the prayer.
