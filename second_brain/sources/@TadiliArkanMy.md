<mark style="background-color: #ff6666">Quote</mark>
> Ta’dil-i arkan means to remain for a while keeping all one’s limbs calm and motionless at five places in salat, namely, at ruku’ (bowing), at two sajdas (prostrations), at qawma (standing upright after ruku’), and at jalsa (sitting between two sajdas).
