# Routine
1. Shower daily <span style="color: red;">but only once</span>. If more than once, then don't scrub too vigorously.
	1. Luke warm water (Good for dry skin).
	2. Schedule after the end of working out.
	3. **Face & Ears**: Gently massage with a [[Hydrating face wash]]. <span style="color: red;">At most once per day</span>
	4. **Hair & Beard**: [[Mild shampoo]] followed by conditioner.
	5. **Body**: 
		   - Use a [[Gentle Body Wash]]. 
		   - Scrub using hands most often. <span style="color: red;">At most twice a week</span>, use a scrubber.
	6. **Feet**: Use feet scrubber.
2. Pad yourself dry, especially face.
	   - Use a dedicated face towel
3. Moisturize within 3 minutes of drying.
	   - **Body**: Use a [[Moisturizer|thick body lotion]].
	   - **Face & Neck**: Use a [[Moisturizer|Face cream]].
	   - **Beard**: Oil
	   - **Lips**: Vaseline
	   - **Feet**: [[Feet cream]]
	   - **[[Sunscreen]]**: If going out. Apply 10 - 15 minutes in advanced.
4. Apply fragrance.
	   - **Armpits**: Roll on
	   - Everywhere else: Apply body spray but only on cloths as alcohol dries the skin.
5. Wash scrubbers with really hot water and disinfectants regularly.
	   - Scrubbers are breading grounds for bacteria. The moist bathroom environment promotes it.

## After Wudu
1. Reapply sunscreen if needed. 
2. Light moisturizer.
