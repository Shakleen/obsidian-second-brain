Many studies show that mouthwashes with fluoride, essential oils, or **chlorhexidine**, used as an adjunct to brushing and flossing, provide additional benefits in reducing plaque, gingivitis, and cavities. However,
- **Chlorhexidine** causes staining.
- Alcohol causes dry mouth.
- Antiseptic rinses wipes out healthy dental bacteria
