# Ghusl Sunnats

1. Wash hands and privates
2. Perform complete ablution/wudu
3. While washing face, intend to perform a ghusl
4. Wash feet
5. Pour water 3 times on head, 3 times on right, 3 times on left.
6. At the end, perform wudu again.
