# Mustahab

## Level: Recommended / Encouraged
These acts strengthen our fard acts and build spiritual resilience. <span style="color:red;">However, completely abandoning these acts while claiming to love Allah can be a sign of hypocrisy</span>.  Included religious acts are:
* [[Sunnat-e-Muakkadah]]
* [[Sunnat-e-Ghair-Muakkadah]]
* [[Nafl]]
## Examples
1. Sunnah prayers
2. Saying Bismillah before eating
3. Visiting the sick
4. Fasting on Mondays and Thursdays
