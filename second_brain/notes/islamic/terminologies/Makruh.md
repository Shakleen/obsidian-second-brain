## Level: Disliked / Unadvised
Makruh actions are **disliked but not sinful** unless constantly practiced or associated with bad intentions. Avoiding Makruh acts brings reward, but doing them does not carry sin
## Examples:
1. Eating garlic before going to the mosque
2. Praying while needing the toilet
3. Wasting water during wudu
