## Level: Neutral
These are actions that are neither encouraged nor discouraged. You're free to do them or leave them-no reward or punishment is attached.
## Examples
1. Choosing your clothing style (as long as it follows modesty guidelines)
2. Eating around a table vs. sitting on the floor
3. Having hobbies or personal routines
