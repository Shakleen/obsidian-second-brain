## Part 1: Ask for Forgiveness
Say the following 3 times (An-Nasaâ€™i (1145))
> Rabb ighfir li 
> 
> Lord Forgive me
## Part 2: Dua
Say the following Dua which was said and recommended by the Prophet (SA) while in the [[Jalsa Al-istira'hah|Jalsa]] position. (Sahih At-Tirmizi 1/90, Sahih Ibn Majah 1/148)
> Allaahum-maghfir lee, warhamnee, wahdinee, wajburnee, wa 'aafinee, warzuqnee, warfa'nee.
> 
> O Allah forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, support me, protect me, provide for me and elevate me
