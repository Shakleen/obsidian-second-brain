Said as a prayer after [[Ehram]] is in effect.
## Transliteration
Allahumma inni asaluka rijika wal jannata, wa awuju bika min gayibika
## Meaning
O Allah! I want to please You and want Jannat. And I want to be free from your displeasure and Jahannam.
