Ijteba is a way to wear [[Ehram clothes]] for [[Tawaf-e-Omrah]].

## Process
1. Take the middle of your clothing
2. Pass it below your right underarm
3. Then cover your left shoulder with it. In this process, your right should will remain exposed.

> [!Tip] Do
> Only during Tawaf which has [[Sayi]].

> [!Error] Don't
> For [[Nafl]] Tawaf,
> Any other time
