## Process of getting into [[Masjidul Haram]]
* Enter through any door, but it is [[Mustahab]] to enter through **Babus Salam** or 24 number door.
* While entering recite [[Talbiya]] repeatedly.
* Pay attention to the [[#Sunnats of entering mosque]]
### Sunnats of entering mosque
* Take off shoes before entering the mosque.
	* If you're going to take the shoes with you, make sure they are clean.
	* First take off your left shoe, then right.
* Say [[Bismillah]] before entering.
* Recite [[Durud]] and [[Salam]].
* Perform [[Niyat]] for [[Nafl]] [[Itekaf]]
* If you look straight, you'll see [[Baytullah]]. 
	* Upon seeing it, immediately recite the following **3 times**:
	  > Allahu akbar la ilaha illallahu
	  > Allah is almighty and there is no god except Allah.
	* This is the time to perform whatever dua you want. It's one of the places where dua is accepted.
