Move to your right until [[Hajre Aswad]] is directly in front of you. 
1. Raise your hands to your ears or shoulders
2. Recite
   > Bismillahi wallahu akbar, wa lillahil hamd, was' salatu was' salamu ala rasulillahi sallallahu ta'la alaihi wassallam
   > **Meaning**: I'm praying for help in the name of Allah (swt). Allah is the greatest. There is no god except for Allah. And durud and salam is for Allah's rasul.
3. Lower your hands.
4. Now [[Kiss Hajre Aswad]].
