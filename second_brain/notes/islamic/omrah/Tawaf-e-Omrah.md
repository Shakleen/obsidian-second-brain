## Preparation
1. The entire process must be completed while [[Wudu]] is in effect. If for whatever reason, your [[What breaks Wudu|wudu breaks]], [[2025-10-30_Complete-wudu|redo wudu]]. 
	> [!Info] What if my [[What breaks Wudu|wudu breaks]]?
	>  - Within the first 3 [[Tawaf round|rounds]], then it's [[Mustahab]] to start from the beginning and do 7 [[Tawaf round|rounds]]. 
	> - After first 3 rounds, then you should just complete the remaining [[Tawaf round|rounds]].
2. [[Enter Masjid-ul-Haram]]
3. Before starting, males should do [[Ijteba]]. ([[Sunnat-e-Muakkadah|Sunnat]])
4. Stop reciting [[Talbiya]]. 
	> [!Error] Don't
	> Until your [[Ehram]] is done, don't recite it again.

## Process of Tawaf
1. Face [[Baytullah|Qaba]] keeping [[Hajre Aswad]] to your right shoulder.
2. [[Niyat]] for tawaf.
3. [[Starting at Hajre Aswad|Start at Hajre Aswad]]
4. Now start [[Tawaf round]] which completes after reaching [[Hajre Aswad]].
	1. Perform 7 total rounds, and end with [[Kiss Hajre Aswad|kissing Hajre aswad]] for the eighth time.
5. After finishing all rounds perform [[Dua at Multajam]].
6. Perform [[Salat-ul-Omrah]].
7. [[Dua at Zamzam Well]]

> [!Error] Don't(s)
> 1. Don't run. Steadily walk.
> 2. If it's time to pray, then pray first. Then continue. 
> 3. In any circumstance, don't bother or hurt anyone or anything. If you do, your [[Omrah]] or [[Hajj]] is invalid.
