1. Hazrat Abu Hurayra (RA) reported,
   > Prophet (SA) said, "One who performs a [[Hajj]] that is free from [[Major Sin|sin]] and bad deeds gets Jannah as prize." (Bukhari, Muslim)
2. Hazrat Abu Hurayra (RA) reported,
   > Prophet (SA) said, "A person who performs [[Hajj]] to please Allah (Swt) and abstains from speaking and doing bad deeds and abstains from [[Major Sin]] and [[Minor Sin]]; then that person becomes as pure as the day he was born from his mother." (Bukhari, Muslim)
3. Hazrat Abu Hurayra (RA) reported,
   > Prophet (SA) said, "If you do one [[Omrah]] after another one, all your sins between the two Omrahs are forgiven." (Bukhari, Mulsim)
4. Hazrat Bara Ibn Azib (RA) reported,
   > Prophet (SA) said, "An [[Omrah]] during ramadan is as valuable as doing a [[Hajj]]" (Bukhari, Muslim)
