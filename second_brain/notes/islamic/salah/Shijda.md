One goes into this position from [[Qawma]] upon hearing [[Takbir]].
## Process going to Shijda
There is a process of going to Shijda which is [[Sunnat-e-Ghair-Muakkadah|Sunnah]]. The following sequence matters when touching the ground:
1. Firstly the kness
2. Secondly the palms of your hands
3. Thirdly the forehead
4. Finally the nose
## Shijda position
1. Forehead and nose kept in contact with the ground
2. Keep two hands by the ears. <span style="color: red;">Do not let forearms touch the ground</span>
3. Keeping toes straight and pointed to the [[Qibla]]
4. Knees bent and touching the ground
5. Keeping back straight
