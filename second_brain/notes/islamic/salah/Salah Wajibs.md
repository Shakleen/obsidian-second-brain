**14** Salah [[Wajib|Wajibs]]
1. Recite [[Sura Fatiha]]
2. Recite one other Sura (at least 3 ayaats)
3. Recite [[Sura Fatiha]] before other Sura(s).
4. [[Tadeel Arkan]]
5. [[Qirat]] should be done in the first 2 [[Rakat|Rakats]] of Fard prayers
6. Maintain order of [[Qirat]], [[Ruku]], and [[Shijda]]
7. First [[Tashahud]]
8. Reading [[Attahyyatu]] in both [[Tashahud|Tashahuds]]
9. [[Tashahud]]
10. Reciting loud or quietly when required
11. Salam to end prayer
12. Reciting [[Dua Qunood]] for [[Bitr prayer]]
13. Six additional [[Takbir|Takbirs]] in [[Eid prayer]]
14. Readng [[Qirat]] loudly for [[2025-10-30_Fajr-prayer|Fajr]], [[2025-10-30_Magrib-prayer|Magrib]], and [[2025-10-30_Isha-prayer|Isha]] for the Imam.
