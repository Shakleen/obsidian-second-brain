This is perhaps the most well known [[Opening Supplication]] that comes after [[Takbir e Tahrima]] is salah.
## Lines
1. Subhanaka Allahumma wa bihamdika
   > سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ وَبِحَمْدِكَ
   > O Allah, glory and praise be to you.
2. wa tabarakasmuka
   > وَتَبَارَكَ اسْمُكَ
   > and blessed be your name
3. wa ta’ala jadduka
   > وَتَعَالَى جَدُّكَ
   > and supreme is your majesty
4. wa la ilaha ghairuk
   > وَلاَ إِلَهَ غَيْرُكَ
   > and there is no God but you
