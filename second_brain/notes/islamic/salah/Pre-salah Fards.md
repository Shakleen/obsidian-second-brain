**Seven** pre-conditions of [[2025-10-28_Salat|Salah]]
1. Clean body through [[Wudu]] and/or [[2025-10-28_Fard-Ghusl|Ghusl]] depending on context.
2. Clean clothing
3. Properly covering body with clothing
4. Clean prayer place or praying mat
5. Facing [[Qibla]] direction
6. Correct time for Salah
7. [[Intention of Salat|Salah niyat]]
