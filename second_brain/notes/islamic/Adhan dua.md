# Adhan dua

After [[Adhan]] completes, recite the following in order:
## First
Wa ana ashahadu alla ilaha illallahu, wadahu la shari kalahu, wa anna muhammadan abduhu wa rasulahu. raditu billahi rabban, wa bi muhammadan rasulan, wabi islami dinan.
> وَأَنَا أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَّا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهُ ، وَأَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُوْلُهُ ، رَضِيْتُ بِاللهِ رَبًّا ، وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ رَسُوْلًا ، وَبِالْإِسْلَامِ دِيْنًا
> I also bear witness that there is no god worthy of worship but Allah. He is Alone and He has no partner whatsoever, and that Muḥammad (SA) is His servant and His Messenger. I am satisfied with Allah as my Lord, with Muḥammad as my Messenger, and with Islam as my religion.

![[Adhan Dua 1.mp3]]
## Second
Allahumma salli ala muhammadeen, wa ala ali muhammadeen
> اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَىٰ مُحَمَّدٍ ، وَعَلَىٰ آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ

![[Adhan dua 2.mp3]]
## Third
Allahumma Rabba hadhihidda‘watit-tammati  wassalatil qa’imati, ati Muhammada-nil-wasilata  wal-fadilata waddarajatar-rafi‘ata, wab‘ath-hu  maqamam-mahmuda-nilladhi wa’attahu. Innaka la  tukhliful mi‘ad.
> اَللّٰهُمَّ رَبَّ هٰذِهِ الدَّعْوَةِ التَّامَّةِ وَالصَّلَاةِ الْقَائِمَةِ ، آتِ مُحَمَّدًا الْوَسِيْلَةَ وَالْفَضِيْلَةَ ، وَابْعَثْهُ مَقَامًا مَّحْمُوْدًا الَّذِيْ وَعَدْتَّهُ

![[Adhan Dua 3.mp3]]
## Forth
Finally make dua. The Prophet (SA) said,
> “Duʿā’ is not rejected between the adhān and the iqāmah” (Tirmidhī).
