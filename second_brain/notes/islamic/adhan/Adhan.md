Adhan is call to prayer. It's given by the Muazzen. 
## Sentences
1. Allahu akbar
   > اللهُ أَكْبَرُ
   > Allah is the Greatest.
2. Ashahadu Allah elaha illallah
   > أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَّا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللهُ 
   > I bear witness that there is no god worthy of worship except Allah.
3. Ashahadu Anna Mohammadur Rasulullah
   > أَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَّسُوْلُ اللهِ 
   > I bear witness that Muḥammad is the Messenger of Allah.
4. Haiya a'lal salat ^fourth-verse
   > حيَّ عَلَى الصَّلَاة
   > Come to salat.
5. Haiya a'lal falah ^fifth-verse
   > حَيَّ عَلَى الْفَلَاح 
   > Come to success.
6. Allahu akbar
   > اللهُ أَكْبَرُ
   > Allah is the Greatest.
7. La elaha illallah
   > لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللهُ 
   > There is no god worthy of worship except Allah.
